Month-Year Mode Matrix

What is a Month-Year Mode Matrix?

This generator outputs an excel workbook with two sheets: the first sheet displays the Media Row IDs and their ridership for every month-year (ex.January 2025), and the second sheet displays the same, except further broken down by transit and rail taps. Totals are included.
